Frequently Asked Questions

Data Delay

  • We use real market data that is 15 minute delayed data

How to Win

  • Return the best total portfolio by the end of the game compared to your competition
  • You do not need to sell your positions at the end of the game to get credit. Your total portfolio will be calculated with the final market prices for each stock that you buy plus your total cash amount at the end of each game
  • All trades are virtual

Available Securities

  • We offer any stock or ETF that is available on your country's exchange. However, we have eliminated illiquid stocks (those that have too low of a combination of volume, price and market cap) and you will not be able to add these to your portfolio
  • If you see a stock that is ineligible for trading but you think it shouldn't be, please contact us

Game Types

  • Users can choose which game they would like to participate in. Each game offers a varied number of players and time duration (i.e. 2 players, 10 players, 1 day, 1 week)
  • Users are limited to one entry per game
  • You can join the same game offering once the current one you joined gets filled

Game Start/End

  • A game will start as soon as the game fills with the required number of players. If a game begins during the middle of a trading day that first day will be considered "extra" time and your game will last for that current day plus the total number of days the game is specified for (i.e. 1 day game that begins mid-day Thursday will last until market close on Friday)
  • On the "live" tab you can see how much time is left until the game ends
  • When you select a game you are participating in you can

Trades

  • We offer unlimited trading. You can buy and sell as frequently as you like
  • Use the in game timeline tab to see your history of when you bought and sold stocks
  • On the Portfolio page you can see the current stock price and how much money you made on that trade since you bought the stock

Order clearing

  • When a trade is placed it will process in 15 minutes
  • Trades are processed in batches. You can only place one trade at a time made up of as many stocks of your choosing.
  • You cannot delete a trade once it has been placed
  • If you place an trade that has not cleared yet, and decide to place a new trade, the old trade will be deleted
  • If your trade does not clear by the time the market closes your trade will clear at the beginning of the next market day and you will buy the stock at the opening price of the next market day
  • A little blue symbol will appear on games in your "live" and "upcoming" tabs if there is an order pending in that particular game
  • You can see the exact time an order will clear on your portfolio tab within each game

Cash Prizes

  • Cash prizes are coming soon. Please check back with us.

Eligibility

  • Anyone is eligible to play our free games
  • For cash games, you must be 18 years or older
